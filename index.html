SummerCamp 2016

Bay Area & NYC

SummerCamp is a group of Harvard students interested in tech and interning in the Bay Area & NYC working to connect other interning students with each other, companies, and alumni.

Last year our membership consisted of 150+ Harvard students interning in the Bay Area. Through SummerCamp, they were able to connect with future friends, future mentors, and potential coworkers.


This year we've partnered with even more incredible organizations. We don't want to say too much yet, but you'll be getting invitations to dinners with founders, meeting alumni, and touring tech campuses. We're also organizing a bunch of social events and outings.


We can't wait to meet you!


Here's to a summer to remember,
