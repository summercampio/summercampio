Welcome!

SummerCamp connects students with each other, companies, and alumni.

Hi! Nice to meet you!

We're SummerCamp, a group of Harvard students that organize events for other students interning in SF and NYC, with the goal of introducing students to: (1) each other, (2) companies, and (3) alumni.

Last year, our membership consisted of 150+ Harvard students interning in the Bay Area and we held events with: Google, Facebook, Sequoia Capital, Highland Capital Partners, Lightspeed Venture Partners, Stripe, Pinterest, and Airbnb.

We're excited to announce that we'll be running again this year! And we can't wait to meet you.

Looking forward to the summer,
The SummerCamp '16 Team
