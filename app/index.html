SummerCamp is a community of Harvard interns in the Bay Area and NYC who come together every summer to celebrate technology.

Over the last two years our membership has consisted of 200+ students interning in both cities. Through SummerCamp, they were able to connect with future friends, future mentors, and potential coworkers.


We facilitate this by organizing dinners, brunches, mixers and campus tours with leading technology companies. Check out some of our previous partners.


Membership is as simple as joining a mailing list, and you're always welcome to unsubscribe.


We can't wait to meet you!

Who is this group open to?

It's primarily focused on Harvard College undergraduates, but our larger events are open to Harvard students (of all schools) / faculty / alumni.

San Francisco Bay Area and New York City.

What are you doing to encourage diversity at your events?

We allocate spots using the following criteria (in order of priority)...

  1. Gender, racial and ethnic diversity
  2. Company-specific requirements (such as concentration, graduation year, etc. though these rarely come up)
  3. First-come first-served

Who runs SummerCamp?

We are a group of current undergraduates who are interning ourselves. We are dedicated to improving the experience of our peers as they intern at various companies; you won't find our names on this website because we believe that this is about the community, and that the organizers come second -- but if you need to get in touch, please feel free to reach out using the email on the contact page and we'll respond ASAP.
